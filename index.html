Sticky Header Inside a Scrolling Div

Content Section

Scroll down this container. The header will stick to the top of this box, not the window.

Most importantly: The `window` and `document` are not scrolling. Therefore, `document.documentElement.scrollTop` will always be 0.

How to Verify:

  1. Open the Developer Console (F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I).
  2. Scroll up and down this page.
  3. In the console, type `document.documentElement.scrollTop` or `window.pageYOffset` and press Enter.
  4. Notice that the value is always `0`, even though you have scrolled.
